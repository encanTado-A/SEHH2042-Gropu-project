code version control guide
purpose: easy retrive of past code, record of progress

!!! reminder: DO YOUR COMMENTING ON YOUR CODE. NO ONE BUT YOU WILL UNDERSTAND THE CODE!!!

### When to do backup ###
when you do any **update** in the program

Update define:
For every big update, update the version (V) by 1
big update include:
class added, function added

For every small update, update the verson (V) by 0.1
small update include
(object) function changed
